# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Guo Zilong ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use the ZestCam app ("the App").

## 1. Overview

ZestCam is a camera application designed to capture photos and Live Photos. The core capture experience works entirely on-device. We do not upload, store, or transmit the photos or Live Photos you capture with the App to our servers.

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal contact details in order to use the App.

## 2. Information We Do Not Receive or Collect

We do not receive, collect, or store on our servers:

- the photos or Live Photos you capture with the App;

- your full photo library contents;

- any location data (the App does not request or use location services);

- any usage or analytics data;

- any device information for tracking or analytics purposes;

- any information about your usage patterns or behavior.

## 3. Information We Do Not Use

The App does not:

- use any third-party analytics or tracking services;

- collect or use any device identifiers for advertising or analytics purposes;

- require or access location services;

- use any third-party libraries that collect user data;

- process or store any data related to your usage of the App;

- share any information with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

## 4. Permissions and Device Access

| Permission | Purpose | How We Handle It |
|---|---|---|
| Camera | Capture photos and Live Photos within the App. | Core functionality; captured media is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. |
| Photo Library (Add) | Save captured photos and Live Photos to your device library. | Used locally on your device only. |
| Photo Library (Read) | Display the most recently saved photo as a thumbnail in the App. | Not used to import your library for editing, processing, or any other purpose. |

The App does not request location permission and does not use location services.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

All media processing and storage occurs on your device. Photos and Live Photos you capture are saved directly to your device's local storage (for example, Apple Photos library). These files are under your sole control and are never transmitted to our servers.

Any backup or sync services you choose to use (such as Apple Photos, iCloud, or other cloud services) are governed by the respective service provider's policies, not ours.

## 6. Photo Metadata

When you capture a photo or Live Photo with the App, the file may contain standard camera metadata such as device model, date, time, and camera settings. The App does not add location metadata to captured media since we do not request location permission.

You are responsible for reviewing and managing the metadata included in your photos when you store, backup, export, or share them using your device or third-party services.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age at which parental consent is required under applicable law. We do not knowingly collect photos or other personal information from children through the App. We do not ask users to create an account or submit direct-contact information in order to use the App.

If you believe a child has provided us personal information through another channel, please contact us and we will review the request and take appropriate action.

## 8. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or tracking purposes.

Apple processes in-app purchases and related billing through your Apple ID account. We do not directly receive your full payment card information from Apple.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" above and may provide notice in the App or through the App Store. Your continued use of the App after the updated Privacy Policy becomes effective constitutes your acknowledgment of the revised Policy.
